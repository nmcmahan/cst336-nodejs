Welcome to Jupiter

This is the largest planet in our solar system. It is the fifth planet in order from the sun, and is a massive gas giant, which might have a solid core. The planet takes only ten hours to rotate once, but takes twelve years to orbit the sun. The atmosphere is mostly hydrogen and helium, and the planet has a massive storm on the surface that has continued for centuries, and has a system of rings, though they are very faint.
