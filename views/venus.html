Welcome to venus

Venus is the second planet from the sun, and is the closest other planet to earth. Despite being further from the sun than mercury, the planets atmosphere traps heat very efficiently, making it the hottest planet. One rotation of venus takes 243 earth days, and one year takes 225 earth days. The planet also rotates backwards compared to other planets.
